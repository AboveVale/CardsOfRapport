Rules and Tips

  1. The rules here are pretty self explanatory, so I'll just give some tips for you.
  2. Press f11 (or fn+f11) on your keyboard to full-screen the game. It's more immersive this way.
  3. Some cards call for: pen & paper, sharpies, crackers, milk, alcohol, 'treat' food, and 'punishment' food. Do with this what you will.
  4. Some cards also call to punish others with 'nasty shots'. These shots are intentionally made to make someone regret choosing to play this game with you. Add spices, sauces, foods, and more to a base liquid to make something truly abhorrent.
  5. You can save and load your game with the toolbar up top. Click in the dropdown to load a save file.
  6. This game was made by a queer person to play with their queer friends, so some questions will assume you are a lil gay. That said, 99% of the questions in this game have nothing to do with gender or sexuality, but go ahead and drink if you aren't queer.
